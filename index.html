Matt & Katrina

July 1, 2017
Woodland Park, Colorado

Our Story

Matt is a diehard Texan, that doesn't own a cowboy hat and Katrina is a Colorado girl who doesn't like snow, but between his affinity for technology and her love of crafts they have no problem building their life together. Thanks to their military service, they initially met while in Qatar, where they managed to see all the sites in Doha. Their first date was Sushi which remains a main stay for date nights. This would be the beginning of a long road ahead.

Back to America

After they redeployed they spent a full year of long distance dating with lots of flights from South Carolina to Texas and vice versa, being in the air would soon become a norm to Matt as he starts taking on bigger roles and responsibilities with work. The 1,000 miles that seperated them would prove to be difficult but they enjoyed the opportunity to travel to new places and new things. They were able to finally be in the same state when Katrina got stationed at Joint Base Lewis-McChord, WA. Matt had the option to stay with his company and work remotly from the comfort of his home.

The Pacific Northwest

Thanks to a mutual love of outdoor activities, delicious sea food, and craft beer the two have loved Washington and everything the Pacific Northwest has to offer. On Valentine's Day of 2016, Matt gave Katrina a super shiny ring, he proposed on the water in Seattle and they celebrated with a trip to the original Starbucks and a fancy dinner, because nothing is better than new jewelry, good coffee and tasty food. When Katrina leaves the Army in December 2016, they will start on the next adventure leading up to tying the knot in July 2017.
